  3. Line a 13 x 18-inch baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. In the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with the paddle attachment of beaters, combine the butter and both sugars.
  5. Beat on medium speed (or high speed if using a hand mixer) for 3 minutes, or until light and fluffy, scraping down the sides and bottom of the bowl as necessary.
  6. Add the salt and baking soda and beat briefly until evenly combined, then add the flour.

Recipe For This Amazing Chocolate Chip Cookie Starts Like This

    1. Ingredients

  • 2 sticks (1 cup) unsalted butter, at room temperature.
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar.
  • 3/4 cup (packed) dark brown sugar (fine to substitute light).
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda.
  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour, spooned into measuring cup and leveled off.
  • 2 cups (12 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips, best quality such as Ghriardelli.
